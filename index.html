Will StudyArc work if my timetable changes every week? ›

Yes. You can mark classes or shifts as "flexible" and StudyArc will keep your core study blocks anchored while letting you shuffle the rest with one drag. Adaptive reminders follow your changes instead of creating duplicates.

Can I use it alongside Notion or paper notes? ›

Absolutely. Many students keep long-form notes in Notion or on paper and use StudyArc purely for planning, reminders and short contextual notes that link back to those systems.

Does it work offline? ›

On desktop browsers, your current week and notes are available offline. Changes sync once you’re back online, so you can plan on the train or in low-connectivity libraries.

Is there a team or class version? ›

We’re testing shared templates and class packs with a small group of universities. If you’re a tutor or coordinator, reach out and we’ll help you onboard your group.
